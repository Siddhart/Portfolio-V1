Star Bot

Star Bot was a little project that i had. It was the first Discord bot that i made.





Invite!


Description:

User1: Does someone know how i can invite my friend to this server?

User2: Yeh you just click on the server name and click invite, Send this link to your friend.

User1: Ah thanks man!

User1: SB star @User2


This bot gives a user a star for appreciation.


There are 6 roles:

- 5 ⭐️'s

- 25 ⭐️'s

- 50 ⭐️'s

- 75 ⭐️'s

- 250 ⭐️'s

- ⭐️ Master


Features:

- Adds roles automatically.

- SB help to get all the commands.

- SB star @user to give someone a star.

- SB check @user to check the amount of stars a user has.
